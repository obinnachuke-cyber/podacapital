About

What is poda capital?

poda capital is a public experiment in treating fashion and second-hand goods like capital.
Framework

Buy. Hold or list. Sell or archive.

The project documents how pieces are acquired, managed, and exited with financial discipline. The closet functions like a portfolio. The newsletter functions like the memo layer.

Why it exists

Most resale content shows aesthetics or outcomes. poda capital is built to show process, capital movement, and the logic behind decisions.
